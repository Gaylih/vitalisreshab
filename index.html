Our Core Services

In-Home Support

Tailored assistance designed to help eligible participants live successfully and safely in their own homes, promoting self-reliance and daily living skills.

Family & Alternate Home Care

Comprehensive support for participants living with their families or in an alternate family home, ensuring a supportive and integrated living environment.

Community Integration

We provide the tools and guidance necessary for meaningful participation in the community, enhancing social skills and overall quality of life.

Certified Excellence in Care

At Vitalis Residential Habilitation, we are fully committed to meeting and exceeding state standards. As an approved Medicaid provider certified by the Department of Health and Welfare (DHW), our policies, procedures, and quality assurance programs are built to guarantee the highest level of care.


Our Commitment to Safety and Quality:

  • Fully certified through the DDA-RH compliance process.
  • Strict adherence to IDAPA 16.04.17 health and safety regulations.
  • Comprehensive Quality Assurance program for ongoing excellence.
  • Participant-centered record management and privacy protocols.

Join the Vitalis Team

We are always looking for compassionate, dedicated professionals to join our agency. Because the safety and well-being of our participants is our top priority, we maintain rigorous hiring standards.


  • Rigorous Screening: All employees must pass a comprehensive criminal history clearance through the DHW Background Check Unit.
  • Qualified Professionals: We employ Qualified Intellectual Disabilities Professionals (QIDPs) and expertly trained direct service staff.
  • Continuous Training: We maintain strict employee files and administrative support to ensure our team is always equipped to provide the best care.
